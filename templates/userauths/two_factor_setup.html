Security Tips
For Authenticator Apps:
  • Keep your phone secure and password-protected
  • Don't share screenshots of your authenticator app
  • Consider using a dedicated device for 2FA
For SMS 2FA:
  • Ensure your phone number is current and secure
  • Be aware of SIM swapping attacks
  • Have a backup phone number if possible
